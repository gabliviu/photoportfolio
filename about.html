The earliest memory I have of anything photography related goes back to being about 4-5. My dad was a sailor at the time and as we would be gone for months at a time, we would always receive postcards from all around the world.

Japanese trains, Indonesian country sides, Italian architecture, and other postcard trends of the late 90s and much more than I can remember at the moment. In a way, what I try to achieve through my photography is playing on those memories I have as a child of discovering all of these foreign places through these postcards and try to evoke these feelings that are deeply rooted in me, and perhaps seeing these places with my own eyes.
